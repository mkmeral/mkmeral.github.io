What I am all about.

I'm a Software Development Engineer with 4+ years of experience building AI-powered systems and cloud infrastructure at Amazon. Currently working on Amazon Q Developer, where I architect LLM-based applications serving enterprise users and develop agentic AI systems with safety guardrails.

My journey started with curiosity - questioning how our Sun "burns" without air in space when I was 10, and spending hours learning about fusion before I even knew multiplication. That same curiosity drives me today as I work on cutting-edge AI systems.

From robotics and machine learning research during university to leading enterprise AI deployments at Amazon, I've always been passionate about building technology that makes a real impact. I specialize in cross-functional collaboration, translating complex technical concepts for diverse audiences, and thriving in fast-paced environments.

Interests

AI Agents & LLMs

Building production AI systems has shown me both the incredible potential and critical safety challenges of agentic AI. I'm passionate about developing responsible AI that can take meaningful action in the world while maintaining human oversight and safety guarantees.

Deep Reinforcement Learning

From my early research with swarm robotics to enterprise AI deployment, I've seen how RL can create emergent behaviors that surpass human-designed systems. The combination of curiosity-driven exploration and safety constraints remains a fascinating challenge.

Distributed Systems & Cloud

Building systems that scale from individual users to enterprise deployments requires deep understanding of distributed architecture, event-driven design, and real-time data processing. These challenges become even more complex when AI agents are involved.

Human-AI Collaboration

The future isn't about AI replacing humans, but about creating seamless collaboration between human intelligence and artificial systems. Designing these interactions - from UX to safety controls - is where the real impact happens.
